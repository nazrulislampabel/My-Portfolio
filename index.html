GreenHive – A Platform for Sustainability Enthusiasts

Description:

GreenHive is an interactive website designed to raise awareness and connect individuals around sustainable and eco-friendly practices. The project provides tools, resources, and a community space for those seeking to reduce their ecological footprint and adopt a greener lifestyle.

Key Features:

  • Educational Blog: Articles on eco-friendly trends and sustainable living tips.
  • Carbon Footprint Calculator: Measure and understand your environmental impact.
  • Marketplace: Buy and sell eco-friendly products with secure payments.
  • Online Community: Forums and monthly challenges for sustainable actions.
  • Interactive Maps: Locate recycling points and local eco-events.
  • Personal Dashboard: Track eco-friendly actions and progress.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: React.js, Tailwind CSS
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Other: Geolocation API, Stripe for payment integration

Target Audience

GreenHive is ideal for young adults interested in sustainability, families aiming for a greener lifestyle, and artisans or producers of eco-friendly products.
